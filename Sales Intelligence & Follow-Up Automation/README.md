## Every DM and sales call automatically analyzed, scored, tagged, and followed up without human involvement. 3 workflows (22 nodes) all Running 24/7.

## THE PROBLEM
Sales teams and service businesses were losing leads due to slow response times and lack of conversation intelligence.

* Hours spent manually reviewing DMs across multiple platforms
* No system to identify which leads were worth pursuing
* Sales call insights locked in recordings nobody reviewed
* Follow-ups forgotten or delayed
* No visibility into lead quality or conversation patterns

## THE SOLUTION
Built a fully automated 3-workflow AI sales intelligence system in n8n that analyses every DM and sales call without any human involvement.
Workflow 1 - DM Analysis
Every DM from Instagram, Facebook and Slack is automatically analyzed by Claude AI. The system scores the lead 1-100, detects sentiment, intent, objections and buying signals, tags it HOT, WARM or COLD, and sends an instant alert with a personalized follow-up message.
Workflow 2 - Sales Call Analysis
The moment a call recording is ready in Fathom, Claude analyses the full transcript. It generates a call summary, extracts objections and buying signals, identifies next steps, scores the lead, and delivers a full coaching brief to the sales rep automatically.
Workflow 3 - Daily Report
Every morning at 8am a summary report is delivered covering all DMs analyzed, lead breakdown by tag, average scores, and call performance metrics from the previous day.

## TOOLS USED

* n8n - Workflow orchestration and automation
* Claude AI - Conversation analysis and lead scoring
* Fathom - Call recording and transcript generation (free)
* Instagram - DM capture via webhook
* Facebook Messenger - DM capture via webhook
* Slack - Message capture via Slack API
* Google Sheets - Lead logging and reporting dashboard
* Gmail - Automated alerts and daily reports

## THE RESULTS
* DM response intelligence: Instant - zero manual review
* HOT leads flagged and alerted within seconds
* Every sales call analyzed automatically after recording
* Daily performance report delivered every morning at 8am
* Zero human involvement from DM receipt to follow-up alert
* System runs 24/7 across 3 platforms simultaneously
