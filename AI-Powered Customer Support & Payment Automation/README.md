## From first message to payment confirmation is fully automated, zero human involvement, running 24/7.

## THE PROBLEM

Service businesses were losing customers due to slow response times
and manual payment processes.

- Hours spent answering the same questions repeatedly
- Manual back and forth to collect customer details
- Payment links generated and sent by hand
- Business owners doing admin instead of running their business

## THE SOLUTION

Built a fully automated customer support and payment system powered
by an AI assistant named Jeff.

Jeff handles the entire customer journey from first message to
payment receipt without any human involvement.

### When a customer reaches out:

 - Jeff responds instantly in a conversational tone

 - Explains services and pricing clearly

 - Collects name, email and phone number

 - Generates a personalized Paystack payment link automatically

 - Sends the link directly in the chat

### The moment payment is completed:

- A branded HTML confirmation email fires instantly
- Delivered to both the customer and business owner in real time

## TOOLS USED

- n8n - Workflow orchestration and automation
- Claude AI - Conversational AI and reasoning
- Paystack API - Payment link generation and processing
- Webhooks - Real time payment event listening
- Gmail API - Automated transactional email delivery
- Custom HTML/CSS - Branded confirmation email template

## THE RESULTS

- Response time: Hours → Seconds
- Manual payment link generation: Eliminated completely
- Business owner notified instantly on every payment
- Zero human involvement from first message to confirmation
- System runs 24/7 with no additional staffing cost
