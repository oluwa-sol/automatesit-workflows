## From first enquiry to booked viewing, the whole process is fully automated.
## 5 workflows. 7 tools. Zero manual work.

## THE PROBLEM

A real estate agency was losing leads every single day.

Not because they lacked properties or clients, because by the time someone responded to an enquiry, the lead had already moved on.

- Leads coming in from Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp and the website all managed manually
- No system to identify which leads were serious
- Viewing bookings coordinated by hand
- Follow-ups forgotten or delayed
- New listings announced manually to each interested client
- Agent starting every day with no clear picture of the pipeline
- Hours spent on tasks that should never require a human

## THE SOLUTION

Built a fully automated 5-workflow lead management system that handles the entire client journey from first message to post-viewing follow-up without the agent touching anything.

### Workflow 1 - Lead Capture & AI Qualification

Every enquiry from Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp and the website is captured instantly.

AI analyses the message, scores the lead 1-100, detects intent and urgency, and routes it automatically.

HOT leads get a personalized reply and booking link within seconds.

WARM leads get a follow-up sequence.

COLD leads get a property information pack.

Every lead is logged to the CRM automatically.

### Workflow 2 - Viewing & Post-Viewing Automation

The moment a viewing is booked, a confirmation goes to the client and the agent gets a full brief.

A reminder fires 24 hours before the viewing automatically. Three hours after the viewing, a check-in email lands in the client's inbox asking for feedback and offering next steps.

### Workflow 3 - Property Matching

When a new listing is added, the system searches the CRM for every lead whose property interest matches the new listing and notifies them instantly with full property details and a booking link.

### Workflow 4 - Cold Lead Reactivation

Every 30 days the system finds all cold leads and sends each one a personalized AI-generated re-engagement message based on their original property interest. No lead is ever permanently lost.

### Workflow 5 - Agent Daily Briefing

Every morning at 7am the agent receives a clean summary of active leads, today's viewings, and leads that have had no activity in the last 48 hours. 

When a new listing is added, the system searches the CRM for every lead whose property interest matches the new listing and notifies them instantly with full property details and a booking link.

## TOOLS USED

- n8n - Workflow orchestration and automation
- OpenAI - Lead scoring, qualification and message generation
- Tally.so - Website lead capture form
- Airtable - CRM and lead database
- Cal.com - Viewing booking and scheduling
- Gmail - Automated client and agent communication
- Hoppscotch - Webhook testing and simulation

## THE RESULTS

Lead response time: Hours → Seconds

HOT leads identified and contacted automatically

Viewing confirmations, reminders and follow-ups automated

New listings matched to interested leads instantly

Cold leads reactivated every 30 days automatically

Agent briefed every morning with full pipeline visibility

5 workflows running 24/7 with zero manual intervention
