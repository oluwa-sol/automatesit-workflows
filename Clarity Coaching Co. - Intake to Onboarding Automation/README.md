## This system reduced response time from 19 hours to 60 seconds and eliminated 17.5 hours of weekly admin work.

## THE PROBLEM
Clarity Coaching Co. was losing leads and drowning in admin work.

* 19+ hour average response time to new inquiries
* 17.5 hours per week spent on manual follow-ups, folder creation, and client onboarding
* 8 manual steps between a lead submitting a form and their first call
* High-intent leads going cold before Sarah could respond

## THE SOLUTION
A fully automated 3-workflow system built in n8n that handles everything from lead capture to client onboarding without Sarah (Coach) touching anything.
Workflow 1 - Lead Scoring and Routing
Every form submission is scored by Claude AI and tagged QUALIFIED, HIGH POTENTIAL or UNQUALIFIED.
Each tag triggers a different automated response within 60 seconds.
Workflow 2 - Post-Booking Onboarding
The moment a discovery call is booked, the system creates a Google Drive client folder with 4 subfolders, sends the intake form, notifies Sarah, creates 4 ClickUp tasks with pre-calculated due dates, and sends a booking confirmation to the client.
Workflow 3 - Intake Form Return
When the client submits their intake form, Airtable updates automatically and Sarah receives a full pre-call brief.

## TOOLS USED

* n8n - Workflow automation
* Claude AI - Lead scoring and tagging
* Typeform - Lead capture and intake forms
* Airtable - CRM and lead tracking
* Google Drive - Client folder management
* ClickUp - Task and project management
* Cal.com - Discovery call booking
* Gmail - Automated client communication

## THE RESULTS
Response time: 19 hours → 60 seconds
Admin time saved: 17.5 hours per week
Manual steps eliminated: 8
Workflows built: 3
Nodes: 18
Everything runs automatically while Sarah focuses on coaching
